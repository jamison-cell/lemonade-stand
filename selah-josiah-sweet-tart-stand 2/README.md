# Selah & Josiah's Sweet and Tart Stand

A kid-friendly lemonade stand checkout app.

## What it does

- Small Lemonade: $1
- Large Lemonade: $2
- Candy Straw: $1
- Cookie: $1
- Tip field
- Still Water Donation field
- Cash or Venmo selector
- Cash received and change due
- Daily totals
- Transaction log
- Sends saved sales to your Google Sheet through Apps Script

## Deploy to Vercel

1. Go to https://vercel.com
2. Sign in with Google
3. Click Add New Project
4. Upload this project folder
5. Click Deploy

## Use on iPad like an app

1. Open the Vercel link in Safari on the iPad
2. Tap Share
3. Tap Add to Home Screen
4. Name it Sweet & Tart
5. Open from the iPad home screen

## New fundraising features

- Total Raised Today display
